OOP1(Java)

Project Name:Journey

Journey is an open world 2D arcade game, made with pure java. In this game player can roam around the world cut trees, mine rocks and kill enemies.Player can also throw a special kunai and teleport to it. Player can buy and sell items in shop.Player also gain experience point from killing mobs and use them to level up his stats.

Computer Graphics

Project Name:Endless Racer

Endless racer is a 2D car racing game, made with c++ and openGL. Player have to race in 3 lane road and there will trucks that he have to dodge in order to stay in game.The player will keep scoring as long as he survies.Player can chose from 3 different car of his choice. The more player survies in game, the more games speed increase. The game will store the ten best high-scores in decending order.

OOP2(C#)

Project Name:Virtual Architect

Virtual Architect an architectural software for planning construction in mixed reality, made with Unity Engine and c#. User first have to take picture of land reference, if the picture is not usable then an error message will be shown.After taking an usable picture user can place 3D models of architectural structures on that land reference and move the camera to see them from the angel the user wants.
